A Little About Me

I'm the creator and owner of this page: Miandra Lorewood. It's probably just easier to know me by Lorewood, but hey there! This entire website is a way for me to store all of my creations in one place really, from the world that you'd encounter in my books to the books themselves and what I've made. I'm a hobby writer, for any who are wondering, but I do coding as my main job (hence the website :D).

Now, onto the main points! I'm a writer based in the UK who's absolutely in love with creating and making new things, and when I discovered writing for the first as a kid, you can only imagine how happy I was at being able to do that with just a few words. All of my stories are fantastical in one way or another, even if they may not end up being of fantasy genre - there's always a little bit of magic somewhere in there.
